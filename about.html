About me

 

 

Basic Info

Twenty-one years ago, on the most important day of the year, I - Rory Jacob Snively (rhymes with 'lively') - was born. I grew up in Manhattan Beach, California under the care of my loving parents Heidi and Steve Snively. I spent most of my childhood playing video games and being overly-conscious of my weight. Those were the days.

 

School

I managed to make it through high school mostly oblivious to the technical side of computing. After being accepted into UCLA's school of engineering, I dipped my feet into several areas of study before finally settling on CS. After three years of pootering around, I'm starting to feel somewhat comfortable with this whole "computron" thing.

 

Employment

During high school, I decided to stop being so much of a waste and got my first job at The Kettle. I have learned so much at this restaurant and met so many wonderful people. While it hardly imbued me with any technical skills, it helped me to break out of my shell into the world of adulthood, it keeps me grounded, and it feeds me once a week!

 

Currently, I am a student developer for CCLE, UCLA's primary online course management system. This job teaches me more and more every day, and is part of the inspiration for creating this website.

 

Other Interests

When I'm forced to partake in physical activity, my sport of choice is racquetball, which I attempt to play once a week with my brother and my dad. I also play bass guitar as much as possible.
