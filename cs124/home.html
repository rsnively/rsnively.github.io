Sequencing A Genome with Insertions

As if reconstructing the entirety of the human genome from little scraps wasn't hard enough, can you believe that everyone's isn't the same? Now that we've identified a bunch of SNPs in which people commonly differ, another problem has arisen. Alterations to your genome can include insertions of base pairs into your genome that no one else has.

Project Goals

The purpose of this project is to correctly identify areas in a sampled genome that have undergone insertions of base pairs quickly and effectively. Hopefully, this will mitigate the additional time required to handle insertions when sequencing a genome.
